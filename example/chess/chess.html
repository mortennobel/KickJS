KickJS chess

Chess engine created based on Tom Kerrigan's Simple Chess Program (TSCP) Copyright 1997 Tom Kerrigan. (See full copyright text at the end of the page)

The KickJS chess is more a proof of concept than an full chess game.

Left click to select (and to move). Right click to change view.
